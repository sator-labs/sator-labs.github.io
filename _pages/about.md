Sator Labs offers comprehensive consulting on machine learning research, strategy and execution, including algorithm development, auditing, and statistical analysis, with a focus on responsible AI and compliance. Our clients include FAANG companies, legislative bodies, and startups. 

Sator Labs is founded by [Dr. Luca Belli](https://lucab.phd), the AI Safety Lead for Spring Health. Before that he was a UC Berkeley Tech Policy Fellow and a NIST AI Fellow working on Generative AI,  Red Teaming and AI Standards. Previously, Luca was co-founder and Research Lead for Twitter's Machine Learning Ethics, Transparency and Accountability (META) team, where his publications included “Algorithmic Amplification of Politics on Twitter.” He also collaborates with the European Commission as part of a small expert group for the production of delegated acts for Digital Service Act. He has spoken at Carnegie Mellon University, the Berkeley AI Law Institute, and the ACM Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining, among other venues.
